# SQL 달리기반

**Lv 4. 단골 고객님 찾기**

```sql
SELECT 
	c.CustomerName AS CustomerName,
	COUNT(o.OrderID) AS OrderCount, 
	COALESCE(SUM(o.TotalAmount), 0) AS TotalSpent
FROM 
	Orders o 
	LEFT JOIN Customers c
	ON o.CustomerID = c.CustomerID
GROUP BY o.CustomerName;
```
